About the Photographer

The eye behind the lens

I'm a travel and landscape photographer from India, drawn to the places where light tells a story. From the sacred Himalayan peaks to the ghost town of Dhanushkodi, from Kerala's backwaters to Dubai's towering skyline — I chase moments that most people walk past.

Every photograph I take is a conversation with a place — its history, its silence, its mood. I believe the best frames aren't planned; they happen when you slow down, look closer, and let the world reveal itself.
